Performing Arts and Wustl Clubs.

There are many examples of wonderful performances by student groups at WashU. These groups are a great way to get involved around campus and meet new interesting people. Often these groups are highly specific. Acapella groups are a good example. The group After Dark has some amazing performances and holds tryouts every year. You can listen to them here. Dance groups are also quite common. The WU dance team, JIVE, is a perfect example



You may also choose to get farthur involved in these performing arts groups by taking classes in dance or the arts. This can be done no matter what school you are in. It may be your passion or a way to meet new people but either way it will be fun.
