Professional Life

Jobs and Internships.

The best way to begin your professional life at WashU is to go to the Career Center located in the DUC. They will provide you with a career advisor and help you get a job. They will also make you aware of career fairs or any start ups that are looking for students to help. If you are looking for a summer position you could also talk to your teachers about working for them. They are often happy to help and will guide you in the right direction.

Building a Business at WashU?

Surprisingly enough you can actually own or operate your own business at Washington University. All along the walk back to the South Forty there are student run businesses. Stop by any of them to learn more about how to start one. There are also student organizations like Y's thoughts which helps students fund thier startups.
