Social Life

How to get Involved.

There are many way to get involved in social life at Washington University. There are four great ways to get involved. First clubs, there are are over a hundred clubs at WashU and each is unique. For more on clubs go to our Clubs page. Second Greek Life, Many WashU students choose to be a part of Greek life. It can be a great way to make close friends. To learn more about Greek life at WashU visit our Greek Life page. Third Performance groups, many individuals who are talented and untalented choose to join performance groups at WashU. These can range from acapella to dance to art groups. To learn more about performance groups go here. Finally sports teams at WashU, There are a variety of teams for different skill levels. While varsity teams play at the D3 level, there are many intramural and club sports that one could join. Look at our Athletics page.
