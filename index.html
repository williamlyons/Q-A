How can we help you?

Our site is looking to help anyone who is interested in learning more about Washington University, especially those individuals who are about to enter the school. We have divided the site into three essiantial parts of the WashU Students life: Social, Professional, and Acedemic. By looking into these three aspects We hope that this site can help you get started at WashU.

Some Background about WashU

Founded in 1853, Washinton University is a moderatly sized private research instiution. Many incredible discoveries have been made at WashU including pionnering work on the human gene project as well as the work of nine nobel laureates. The university is made up of seven graduate and undergraduate schools. There are roughly 14,000 students enrolled in these schools. The campus is located in clayton very close to the center of St. Louis.
